Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What is the EcoElite Smart Bin?

The EcoElite Smart Bin is an innovative waste management solution equipped with advanced sensors and a mobile app to help you monitor waste levels, get recycling reminders, and receive tips on reducing waste.

How does the EcoElite Smart Bin work?

Our smart bin uses sensors to detect waste levels and sends this information to your mobile app. You can track your waste, receive notifications for recycling days, and get suggestions on how to manage your waste more efficiently.
